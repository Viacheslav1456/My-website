Part 1 - Political system in Russia

Currently,the main features of the Russian political system include: presidential power, government and federal assembly.
There are also many other powers of the plate system, but they do not have such power as those listed above.




Presidential Power

The prime minister, who leads the government, is chosen by the president, who serves as head of state. In addition, the president
has substantial authority over foreign affairs and oversees the armed forces. Vladimir Putin serves as the Russian Federation's
president at the moment. Dmitry Medvedev was president before him, in addition.




Government

The prime minister and other ministers chosen by the president make up the government, which is in charge of enforcing national policy.
The current Prime Minister of Russia is Mikhail Mishustin. He was appointed to this role in January 2020 and will continue to hold it once
his second cabinet is approved in May 2024.



Federal Assembbly

The Federal Assembly represents Russia's legislative power, and it is made up of two chambers: the Federation Council (upper house) and the State Duma (lower house).



Federation Council

The Federation Council (upper house) represents the country's regions and is made up of senators nominated from each subject in the Russian Federation.

State Duma

The State Duma (lower house) is made up of 450 representatives elected in general elections, and it is in charge of creating and passing legislation.

Part 2 - Political System in India

The Indian political system is a federal parliamentary democratic republic. It comprises the executive, the legislature, and the judiciary.
The President of India is the head of state, while the Prime Minister is the head of government.




Executive Power

The President of India, who is the ceremonial head of state, is elected for a five-year term. The Prime Minister, who is the head of government, is appointed by the President and is usually the leader of the party that has a majority in the Lok Sabha (House of the People).Narendra Modi serves as the prime minister of India and Droupadi Murmur is the president of India.
